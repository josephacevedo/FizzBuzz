Fizz Buzz

Fizz Buzz loops from 0 to 100 and any number that is a factor of the given fizz or buzz value will be displayed as fizz or buzz and will have a distinct color. Any number that does not match the conditions will be displayed as a normal number.

Instructions

Enter a fizz value and buzz value and press run.
